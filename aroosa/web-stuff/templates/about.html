What is this project about?

This project analyses the energy consumption of Australian states from the 2000 to 2018 financial year and compares them to the GDP trends over this time period .

Combining the strengths of the project members, this interactive dashboard thoroughly analyses the trends between energy consumption and GDP of Australia. Various graphs and plots will examine how energy usage has changed within the states of Australia and compare this with GDP data to evaluate any correlations
